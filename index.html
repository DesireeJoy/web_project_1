The main problem with education

Whether it's at school or university, the one thing no one ever teaches you is how to teach yourself. All you actually do is memorize information that you wil regurgitate when you take the test. You get drilled on how to solve specific exercises so that whiel you know exactly what you need to do when exam times comes around, do you truly understand the subject matieral? All you've really learned is how to pass that one test. Once the test is over, the information in your head has outlived it's usefulness.
